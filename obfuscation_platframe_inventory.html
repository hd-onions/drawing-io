*** List of requirements for H&D ***

  • The website about the event:
    • To display basic information like schedule, information about organisers and information about the event as a whole. 
    • To display videos, articles, and other relevant media to be accessible before, during and after the event.
  • Teleconference and interaction capabilities:
    • (Ideally embedded as much as possible on the website).
    • BBB instance for meetings and hands-on workshops: ideally should be enough to enable interaction between a large number (100-150) of participants (audio and video). 
    • Streaming instance (from BBB instance, for people that won't join with video and audio, and in case BBB capacity is not be enough). 
    • In addition to the chat and other interaction features that BBB already provides, it would be amazing to have an integrated public chat, where both BBB and streaming instance chats coalesce. -- but maybe this is asking too much?
    • Have a separate chat or meeting room (independent from BBB) for people to meet during breaks and before/after plenary sessions.
      • Ideally enabling both "public" and private chats (and also selective group chat?).
    • Also need a collaborative editing platform for people to edit together remotely -- a pad functionality, essentially
    • Something like a twitter plugin that embeds content relevant to the workshop from Twitter?
  • Media production support
    • Help speakers record short videos to be made available before event
    • Video edition (cutting, adding text, transitions, etc).
    • Video upload and player facilities?
